Top Attractions

Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty is a symbol of freedom and democracy. Located on Liberty Island, it's one of the most visited landmarks in the US.

Central Park

Central Park is an urban oasis in the heart of Manhattan, perfect for walking, biking, or relaxing among trees and lakes.

Image: Central Park, Pixabay, License info

The Metropolitan Museum of Art

Home to over 2 million works of art, The Met is one of the largest and most prestigious museums in the world.

Times Square

Times Square is the epicenter of entertainment and lights. From Broadway shows to digital billboards, it captures the vibrant spirit of the city.
